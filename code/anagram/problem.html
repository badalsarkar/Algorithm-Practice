Anagram

You are given a word. Generate all the anagrams of that word.

Anagrams of a word means all possible permutations of that word.

Example:

The word "ANT" has following anagrams-
  • ANT
  • ATN
  • NTA
  • NAT
  • TAN
  • TNA
There are total 6 anagrams for the word "ANT".

Loop through the word, take one letter and rearrange other letters.

i=0, letter=A, rearrange "NT" i=0, letter=A, rearrange "TN" i=1, letter=N, rearrange "AT" i=1, letter=N, rearrange "TA" i=2, letter=T, rearrange "AN" i=2, letter=T, rearrange "NA"

Lets simplify the problem. The anagram of 1 letter is the letter only.

Anagram of "A" = "A"
